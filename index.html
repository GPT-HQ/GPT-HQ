Surveys — How to Do Them Correctly

Best survey providers: Based on experience, BitLabs and Prime consistently offer the most reliable surveys, the best routing, and the highest approval rates.

How to answer surveys properly:

  • Always answer honestly whenever possible — it gives you the highest approval rate.
  • If you choose to lie, you must keep the same lie forever. Survey providers store your answers and compare them across all surveys.
  • If your answers don’t match your previous ones with the same survey provider, you can be banned or have rewards revoked.
  • Read every question carefully — rushing increases disqualification.
  • Never contradict yourself on key details (age, job, income, household size, education).
  • Don’t randomly click options — attention checks will catch you instantly.
  • Use consistent demographic info across all platforms and routers.
  • If a survey feels broken or suspicious, leave it instead of forcing answers.

Quick tips to increase approval rate:

  • Keep your profile updated on each survey router.
  • Avoid extreme answers unless they truly apply to you.
  • Take surveys in a quiet environment to avoid mistakes.
  • Don’t speed — slow and steady earns more in the long run.
  • Be patient: some days have fewer good surveys than others.
